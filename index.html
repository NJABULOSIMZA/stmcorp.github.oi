About STM Corporation

Over 12 years of engineering excellence and innovation

STM Corporation is a 100% black owned company formed in 2012, with over 12 years of experience in the engineering and construction industry. We are committed to precision and excellence, providing comprehensive solutions that cater to the unique needs of our clients across diverse industries.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled quality and reliability in Engineering, Mining, Construction, Equipment and consumables. We strive to be a trusted partner for our clients, consistently exceeding expectations through expertise, innovation, and a commitment to sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To become a leading and efficient engineering company in South Africa while creating employment and ensuring sustainability.
